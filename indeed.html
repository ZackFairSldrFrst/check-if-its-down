About Indeed

Indeed is the world's #1 job site, with over 350 million unique visitors every month. The platform aggregates job listings from thousands of sources including company career pages, job boards, staffing agencies, and associations. Indeed helps job seekers find opportunities and employers find qualified candidates across virtually every industry and job type.

For millions of job seekers worldwide, Indeed is an essential tool for searching positions, uploading resumes, and applying to opportunities. For recruiters and HR professionals, it's a critical platform for posting jobs and sourcing candidates.

What Causes Indeed Outages?

Third-Party Infrastructure Issues

The November 18, 2025 outage demonstrated how Indeed's reliance on third-party services like Cloudflare for content delivery can cause accessibility problems. When these infrastructure providers experience disruptions, Indeed becomes inaccessible even when Indeed's own systems are functioning normally.

High Traffic Periods

Indeed can experience heavy load during:

  • Monday mornings when job seekers are most active
  • First of the month (when many new positions are posted)
  • After major company layoff announcements
  • During economic downturns when unemployment rises

Platform Updates and Maintenance

Indeed regularly updates its search algorithms, user interface, and backend systems. While maintenance is typically scheduled during low-traffic periods, updates can occasionally cause temporary service disruptions.

Database and Search Engine Issues

With millions of job listings to index and search, problems with Indeed's database or search infrastructure can affect the ability to find and apply for positions.

Impact of Indeed Outages

For Job Seekers

When Indeed goes down, active job seekers face several challenges:

  • Missed Opportunities: Can't search for or view new job postings
  • Application Deadlines: Unable to submit time-sensitive applications
  • Profile Access: Can't update resumes or profile information
  • Communication: May miss messages from potential employers

For Employers and Recruiters

HR professionals and hiring managers are also affected:

  • Can't Post Jobs: Unable to publish new job listings
  • Candidate Review: Can't access applicant information or resumes
  • Screening Tools: Unable to use Indeed's candidate screening features
  • Communication: Can't message candidates or respond to applications

Recent Indeed Outages

November 18, 2025

Cloudflare-Related Outage: Indeed became inaccessible due to Cloudflare CDN disruption affecting multiple major internet services. The outage occurred during peak job-searching hours (early morning), preventing job seekers from searching and applying for positions, and blocking employers from posting jobs or reviewing candidates. Service was restored as Cloudflare resolved infrastructure issues.

How to Check If Indeed Is Down

1. Use This Status Page: Our free Indeed down detector monitors the platform in real-time and provides immediate updates on detected issues.

2. Test Different Access Methods:

  • Try the Indeed mobile app (iOS or Android)
  • Access from different devices or networks
  • Try different browsers or incognito mode
  • Check if you can access the homepage vs. specific job listings

3. Check for Specific Issues:

  • Can you load the homepage but not search?
  • Does the site load but applications won't submit?
  • Is your employer dashboard accessible?
  • Are you receiving error messages?

4. Social Media and Forums: Check platforms like Twitter/X, Reddit, or LinkedIn for reports from other users experiencing similar issues.

What to Do During an Indeed Outage

For Job Seekers:

  • Use Alternative Job Boards: Try LinkedIn, ZipRecruiter, Monster, or Glassdoor
  • Apply Directly: Visit company career pages to apply without Indeed
  • Save Job Listings: Bookmark positions you wanted to apply for
  • Be Patient: Most outages resolve within a few hours
  • Check Back Here: Monitor this page for real-time status updates

For Employers:

  • Post on Alternative Platforms: Use LinkedIn, company website, or other job boards
  • Document the Outage: Note any impact on hiring timelines
  • Communicate with Team: Inform hiring managers about the disruption
  • Prepare for Recovery: Be ready to review applications once service is restored

Preventing Impact from Outages

Diversify Your Job Search:

  • Don't rely solely on Indeed - use multiple job boards
  • Set up job alerts on various platforms
  • Network on LinkedIn and professional associations
  • Apply directly on company career pages
  • Work with recruiters who may have inside access

For Employers - Multi-Channel Recruiting:

  • Post jobs on multiple platforms simultaneously
  • Maintain your own careers page independent of job boards
  • Build talent pipelines that don't depend on a single platform
  • Have backup processes for candidate communication
